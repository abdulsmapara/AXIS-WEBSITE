WHO’S THE BOSS
Tech, Enthusiasm and much more...


About

A brainy techno-analytical event intended to bring together the critical thinkers and decision makers who constitute the massive pillars of the corporate world. Who’s The Boss is an event which explores the techno-management, soft skills and puts to test the deductive and aptitude skills which are in very much demand in the present world. The complete event consists of three stages, the selected participants from each round proceed to the next round -
The Aptitude Test – Participation: Individual
The Game/ Event – Participation: Team of 3-5 members (formed on-the-spot)
Group Discussion and Personal Interview – with our panel of judges.

Problem Statement

The event consists of 3 rounds in total, the first round which will be a general aptitude test will be open for all. Selected participants from each round will proceed to the next round. The second round will be where the participants will be separated into teams and a game will be played amongst these teams that will test their resource management skills. The third round will consist of a group discussion and further interviews with out panel of judges.

Rules and Regulations

  • Participant must register at www.axisvnit.org.
  • The event is open to all college students free of cost.
  • Open to individual participants and not as a group/ team from any college.
  • Participants need to carry valid ID of their respective institute.
  • The selection for the 2nd round (main round) will be purely based on the eliminations conducted in the 1st round (aptitude test). Same applies to the 3rd round i.e., selection based on the winning teams of 2nd round.
  • The teams formed on-the-spot for the second round by the event organizers and managers on random basis will be final.
  • The decision of the Event Managers shall be regarded and treated as final and binding on all. They have the rights to verify identity of the participants if required.
  • Aptitude Test (1st round) for VNIT students will be conducted offline and for students of other colleges it will be conducted online. In no case shall the results of online round be considered for VNIT students i.e., they must appear for the test offline only.
