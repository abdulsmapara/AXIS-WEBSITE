Kartavya



About

Kartavya is a socio-technical event of AXIS whose main motive is to provide a technical solution to the problems faced by the elite group NGOs as the to-be engineers. The event is one such platform to creative social awareness in the minds of the students to uplift the needy and promote social welfare. If you seek some peace of mind, this is a great chance to give back to the society.
The participants are asked to draft a feasible solution to the major problem of an NGO chosen thus creating a bridge between NGOs and engineers.

Game Play

The event would consist of two rounds:
ROUND 1:
In this round, the participants have to submit an abstract based on the solution for problem statement given. These would be problems faced by NGOs. This problem statement has to be answered in 150-200 words. The solution should be practical and applicable in real life scenario. Abstract should be uploaded to the link kartavya@axisvnit.org and then participants would be short listed for the next round.
ROUND 2:
You have to present a prototype of the solution suggested (a working or a non-working model) on the day of round 2 along with a presentation which would be presented in front of the panel of judges belonging to the NGO. The winner would be selected based on the points scored. Note: Shortlisted people who are from outside Nagpur, can make a video of their presentation and mail it to us by the intended date which will be informed after shortlisting.

Rules

  • All the participants must register for Kartavya at www.axisvnit.org .
  • Number of participants in a group should not exceed 4.
  • Abstract should be mailed to kartavya@axisvnit.org in PDF format.
  • Submission of abstract for round 1 should be on or before 18 th February 2017.
  • The solutions the participants come up with, should be technical, cost efficient and easy to implement.
  • Abstract must contain your personal information such as your name, college name, city, phone number etc.

    • Judging Criteria

  • The judges will make all the decisions purely on the basis of prototype and presentation.
  • Points distribution would be given on the day of round 2.

    • NOTE:In case of any dispute, the decision taken by the AXIS Executive Committee will be final and binding for all.
