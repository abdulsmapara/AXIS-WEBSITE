Problem Statement

This is a 5 hr programming paradise for insomniacs where they will be served with mind boggling coding and algorithmic challenges to solve. There will be flexibility to use any programming language of their choice.

Gameplay

Pre-registration :
Each participant should register themselves for INSOMNIA under bit.ly/AXIS19_insomnia and also have to form a team of 3 members and fill the details of team members at bit.ly/AXISinsomnia_reg

NOTE - Any one of the team member should fill the team form.

The event would consist of two rounds:

Coding Round(Round I):

This is a individual coding challenge which will include coding data structures and algorithmic questions. The duration of the round is 5 hours. Based on their points scored by all team members individually, the top teams will be shortlisted for the next round, Microland Challenge (Round 2).

Link for the coding round-
Cash prizes and goodies will be given to the winners of this coding round.

Rules and Regulations :
  • Participants has to compete in Coding Round(Round I) individually.
  • The Contest will consist of 8 programming questions.
  • The participant will score maximum points on correctly solving all the test cases.
  • Partial marking will be there in case the participant correctly solves some of the test cases.
  • Maximum marks for a question may vary. The level of questions will be a mixture
  • Participants need to complete the contest between 9pm-2am on 10th February 2019.


    • Microland challenge(Round II):

    The selected participants from coding round will get problem statement from Microland, The participants need to solve and submit their presentation and demo within the given deadline which will be communicated. Based on the submission, selected candidates/ teams will have to present their presentation and demo at VNIT to the Microland panel.

    Rules and Regulations :
  • Based on the average scores made by individual participants of a team (as registered in the google form) made in the round 1, teams will be selected for Microland Challenge(Round II).
  • Selected participants will receive problem statement for Round 2 on 14th February 2019.
  • Participants have to solve the problem statement and has to submit their presentation or demo as per the informed deadline
  • Based on the submission the participants will be filtered and called to VNIT for final presentation.
  • Based on the final presentation winners will receiving goodies and internship opportunity at Microland(Worth Rs 1lakh)
  • Eligibility for Microland intern
    1. He/She should be a 2nd or 3rd year B.tech student
    2. There is no pointer or college criteria for intern.

    • SCORING

  • Scoring is based on Speed, Accuracy and Efficiency of the code.
  • Each question has predetermined score.
  • The participants would get partial score only after passing certain number of test cases. To get the full score, all test cases have to be passed efficiently.
  • In case of same scores of participants, the tie is broken by sum of time participant took in submitting the last solution resulting the highest score.
