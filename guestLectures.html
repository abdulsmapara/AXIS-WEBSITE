JAAP HAARTSEN
Jaap C. Haartsen (Feb1963) has been active in the area of Wireless Communications for more than 25 years.In 1994, he laid the foundations for the system that later became known as Bluetooth Wireless Technology. He is internationally regarded as the Father of Bluetooth. In May 2015, he was inducted in the National Inventor Hall of Fame (NIHF) at the USPTO, Washington for his work on the Bluetooth technology.
