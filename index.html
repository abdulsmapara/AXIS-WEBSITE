About Us

You need to see this

AXIS in 2001 jostled all the doubts The enthusiasm and knew no bounds with participation of almost 25,000 school and college students from all over the country . The pace was further only escalated with the eminent personalities from all sectors gracing AXIS with their presence, kind words and invaluable experiences. Dr. Dinesh Keskar( VNIT Alumnus and senior vice president, Boeing Commercial Airplanes) and the late Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam were the well engraved names in the history of AXIS. The technological festival successfully boasts of having associated with big names of industry like NDRF and DRDO. The workshops are well executed and the proshows of AXIS hold onto its reputation by hosting Laser Show, Tron Dance and the memorable performance of the international DJ Afishal throughout the years. AXIS also indulges in social activities with the help of the organisations like Make A Difference and Prayaas, throughout the year.
